Napotalian Pizza

Napotalian Pizza from a small village in the alps of Italy.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Spices
  • Pizza dough from your local Italian dude

Steps

  1. Prepare meat with the spices
  2. Toss it over the whole pizza dough
  3. Smack it in the oven for 5 minutes at 450 celsius
  4. Enjoy!
